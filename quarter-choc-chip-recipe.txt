Ingredients:
1 c 2T flour
1/2 t baking soda
1/2 c margarine
6T brown sugar
2T white sugar
1/2 pckg insta pudding
1 egg
2 t vanilla extract
1 c semi-sweet choc chips

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 175C. Sift together baking soda and flour, set aside.
2. In large bowl, cream together margarine, brown sugar, white sugar. Beat in insta pudding
until blended. Stir in eggs and vanilla. Blend in flour mixture. Finally, stir in choc chips.
Drop cookies by rounded spoonful onto cookie sheet.
3. Bake for 10-12 min.
